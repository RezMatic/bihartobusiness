Manish Roushan - The Author

Manish Roushan is a global authority on Educational Transformation and Business Breakthrough. He is a Communication Scientist and the first in the world to connect education and business together by intregrating both of them on a unified platform which the sheer virtue of communication dynamics and engagement model. He is a national award winner for his contribution to the management education in India. He is a business coach as well as a mentor to various business schools in India.

Manish Roushan researches on the trend of what it takes businesses to prosper initially and what elements when collaborated together produce astronomical results. His major areas of research include TalenTrend(A periodic reserach on the changing dynamics of skills), the changing trend of modern marketing and how professional pattern of education backs both the academic and business ecosystem.

His recent initiatives are Business Backs Learning, a global advocacy campaign that encourages businesses, companies, and corporations to support learning expecially in tier 2 and tier 3 cities and Roushan Experiential Learning (REAL) Program, a learning and participation program for business schools

Manish Roushan is an entrepreneur and his companies work in the field of Technology, Marketing Communication and Consultancy. He is an avid reader and likes travelling to explore and meet new people.
